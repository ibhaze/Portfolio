    Wish 'N Toss

    01

    Goal

    The goal was to solve the problem of parents with too many toys for their kids but not enough space to store them.

    02

    Process

    The project involved three cycles of ideation to find the best approach. Research showed that parents often give away or throw out toys. Through interviews with a parent, I developed the idea of a platform where parents could connect to swap toys they no longer needed. Initially, I considered a feature inspired by Tinder swipes to make the process engaging. However, I decided against it for ethical reasons, as it might become addictive. Instead, I opted for a simple e-store-style feature.

    03

    Tools Used

    • Git
    • Trello
    • Figma
    • Flutter
    04

    Results

    The app allows parents in the North Brabant area to swap toys with each other. Users can find toys, upload images of their own, and specify conditions for exchange. This created a new platform for a toy marketplace app, linking to the Dutch tradition of Sinterklaas gift-giving.
