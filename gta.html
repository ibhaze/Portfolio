    Game Tutorial Alliance

    01

    Goal

    The goal was to produce comprehensive yet easy to grasp tutorials for student gamers

    02

    Process

    I brainstormed with my group to see what a good project to tackle would be and it was glaring that easy-to-understand gaming tutorials didn't exist at the time , so this was the project , after this , I interviewed Fontys students to see what the most popular games in the school were and I designed a website that had tutorials on only those games.

    03

    Tools Used

    • Git
    • Trello
    • Figma
    • HTML/CSS
    • Javascript
    04

    Results

    The website creates an opportunity for student gamers to view tutorials on the games they like . The website contains both audio and video tutorials that are both short and direct , making the website a gamers dream.
